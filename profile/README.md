# AIA Agents

The [AI Alliance](https://thealliance.ai), is a community of technology creators, developers, and adopters collaborating to advance safe, responsible AI rooted in open innovation.

The AI Alliance is focused on accelerating and disseminating open innovation across the AI technology landscape to improve foundational capabilities, safety, security and trust in AI, and to responsibly maximize benefits to people and society everywhere.

This organization is intended to house GenAI agents built in conjunction with the AI Alliance affiliated [Gofannon project](https://github.com/The-AI-Alliance/gofannon).
